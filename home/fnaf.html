The Five Night at Freddy's (FNAF) timeline is complex, spanninng decades across games and books that exist in seperate, though parallel, continuities. The following details the game continuity using evidence from the sources.

Book Continuity

The main timeline of events depicted or referenced in the games and in-game books is as follows:

1979

Fall Fest

The Mimic endoskeleton and Mimic1 program are created by Edwin Murray. The Mimic learns violent behavior after witnessing trauma and is later repurposed by Fazbear Entertainment, eventually influencing later events including Help Wanted and Security Breach.

Late 1970s-Early 1980s

Fredbear's Family Diner

Fredbear's Family Diner is founded by William Afton and Henry Emily. A very early stage show known as Fredbear’s Singing Show may have existed prior to the diner. Henry's daughter, Charlotte (Charlie), is murdered by William outside the diner, and her soul possesses the Puppet animatronic.

1983

Bite of '83

Events of Five Nights at Freddy's 4 minigames take place. William Afton's younger son (the Crying Child/Evan) suffers the "Bite of '83" from the Fredbear animatronic during his birthday party and later dies. The in-game TV displays a "Fredbear and Friends" commercial with the date 1983, confirming the year.

Mid-1980's

MCI

The Missing Children Incident (MCI) occurs at a Freddy Fazbear's Pizza location, where William murders at least five children, whose souls go on to possess the main animatronics (Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, Foxy, and possibly Golden Freddy).

Mid-1980's (Shortly after the MCI)

Circus Baby's Pizza World

Circus Baby's Pizza World opens as a sister location under Afton Robotics. Elizabeth Afton is killed by Circus Baby shortly after opening, causing the restaurant to immediately shut down. The animatronics are later moved to Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rental (CBEAR).

1987

Bite of '87

The gameplay of Five Nights at Freddy's 2 occurs. A second set of murders (often referred to as the "Save Them" incident) is implied to take place at this location. The "Bite of '87" happens during the day, which Phone Guy mentions in the first game, leading to animatronics being restricted from walking during the day.

Early-to-mid 1990s (c.1993)

FNAF game

The gameplay of the original Five Nights at Freddy's takes place. The location is the last iteration of the classic Freddy's pizzerias before a long closure. The date is approximated by the minimum wage listed on Mike Schmidt's paycheck.

Late 1990s (Post-FNAF 1 Closure)

Springlock Incident

William Afton returns to the abandoned Freddy Fazbear's Pizza location and dismantles the original animatronics. The spirits of the murdered children corner him, forcing him to hide inside the Spring Bonnie suit. A springlock failure occurs, killing him and trapping him inside the suit, creating Springtrap.

Unspecified Time (Post-1993, Pre-2023)

Sister Location/CBEAR

Five Nights at Freddy's: Sister Location takes place at Circus Baby's Entertainment and Rental (CBEAR). This location is run by Afton Robotics. William's daughter, Elizabeth Afton, was killed by Baby years prior. William's son, Michael, eventually goes there to find his father, is "scooped," and becomes a host for the Ennard amalgamation. Some interpretations suggest that the remnant from the original animatronics was used in the Funtime animatronics (MoltenMCI theory).

2023

Fazbear's Frights

Five Nights at Freddy's 3 takes place thirty years after the last Freddy Fazbear's Pizza closed its doors. William Afton, trapped in the Spring Bonnie suit (Springtrap), is found and moved to the horror attraction Fazbear's Fright. The attraction burns down.

Shortly After (2023)

Pizza Simulator

Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria Simulator (FNaF 6) happens. Henry Emily sets a final trap to lure the remaining haunted animatronics (Molten Freddy/Ennard, Springtrap/William, Scrap Baby/Elizabeth, and Lefty/Puppet/Charlie) into a single location and burn them all, including himself, to end the cycle. The Midnight Motorist minigame suggests additional backstory involving William Afton and a runaway child.

Post-2023

UCN

Ultimate Custom Night is William Afton's perpetual purgatory/hell, where he is tortured by a vengeful spirit (believed to be Cassidy/Golden Freddy), though some interpretations suggest this may be a remnant-based nightmare state rather than a literal afterlife.

Late 2020s (c. 2025-2029)

Help Wanted VR Game

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted is released by Fazbear Entertainment to clean up their reputation by branding the past events as "fictitious scenarios" in a VR game. The circuit board from the burned animatronics is scanned into the game, creating the Glitchtrap virus. Later evidence suggests Glitchtrap may be connected to the Mimic1 program rather than being William Afton directly. The virus infects a beta tester named Vanessa.

Late 2020s (c. 2025-2029)

Special Delivery

Five Nights at Freddy's AR: Special Delivery occurs as Fazbear Entertainment expands its digital presence.

Early 2030's (c. 2029-2031)

Mega Pizzaplex

Five Nights at Freddy's: Security Breach takes place at the Mega Pizzaplex. Gregory is trapped overnight and helped by Freddy. Vanessa is revealed to be Vanny, controlled by Glitchtrap. Beneath the Pizzaplex, remnants of previous events and a version of Burntrap are discovered.

Early 2030s (c. 2029-2031)

Help Wanted VR Game 2

Five Nights at Freddy's: Help Wanted 2 is set between Security Breach and its DLC, as one ending shows the player character becoming the Maskbot who gives Cassie the V.A.N.N.I. mask.

Early 2030's (C. 2029-2032)

Mega Pizzaplex 2

The Ruin DLC for Security Breach takes place, with a new protagonist, Cassie, exploring the abandoned Pizzaplex and encountering the Mimic animatronic. The Mimic is revealed to be a separate entity capable of copying behaviors and identities, strongly tying into the Tales from the Pizzaplex series.
